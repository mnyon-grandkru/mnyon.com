--- layout: post title: 'Hopstop: Mapquest for NYC people' date: '2005-06-14T22:19:00.000-05:00' author: Mark Nyon tags: modified_time: '2005-06-14T22:22:40.836-05:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-11932736.post-111880576082402758 blogger_orig_url: https://www.mnyon.com/2005/06/hopstop-mapquest-for-nyc-people.html --- Thanks to the Burner list; someone on there dropped the dime on HopStop, a Mapquest based on travel by subway, since most of us in this city don't have a car, and those who do, don't use it. Check it out; makes scheduling travel a lot easier. It evidently even estimates travel based on train speed at the time of day, so it'll take into account the 8AM slowness of the 4 and 5 trains headed downtown.
