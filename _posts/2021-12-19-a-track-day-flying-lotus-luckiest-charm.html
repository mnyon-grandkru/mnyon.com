This song sets a comfortable table, a relaxed mood, a lush sound to settle into my ears. The rhythm subtly thumps methodically, sitting under arresting vocal refrains and strings. It's a nice background song for a place where people can share intimacy through word or deed, even if only for a short time. I constantly marvel at Flying Lotus's range, from frenetic explosiions to songs that sit comfortably in many pockets, like this one.

This isn't a head bop, but more of a sway one's head softly from side to side with the beat. Reminisce about times filled with mystery & intrigue. Catalog lessons learned. Smile at the thought of the future.
