nk☆kn · Love Love Mode DIMITRI FROM PARIS

I found this quote which resonated with me while cruising through social media.

French house music from like 96 - 2000 is on the perfect fault line of camp ridiculousness and deepness. It's not cool but I love that shit. Gonna listen to Super Discount again maybe.

— C.Scott (@CScott412) January 6, 2022

"Love Love Mode" was the first track that came to my mind. The vocal sample non-sequiturs serving as instruments, the accordion & other synths playing various roles on the track, the simple ditties that take center stage at some points of the track. It all comes together in a package that is entertaining and doesn't take itself too seriously. Which is an undervalued quality in music nowadays.

Give it a listen if you're in the mood for something fun and easy on the ears. It's definitely a nice break from news & current events.
