I think I first heard Heed the Word of a Brother by X Clan. Or You Gots To Chill from EPMD. My little brain kept connecting the dots through that bassline.

It was a weekend afternoon; people were playing music on the block while cleaning up the house when I actually heard Roger's voice. A voice with a purpose. A direction. A bounce. A song that is automatic sunshine.
