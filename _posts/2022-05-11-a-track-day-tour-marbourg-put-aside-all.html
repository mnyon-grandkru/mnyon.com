It's been a minute and five-halves. Insert Rakim quote here.

While traveling and keeping my sanity as the world worlds, I've been continuing to enjoy Kiffen Beats mixes a lot. One evening, I was journaling and thinking over past memories and regrets, spending little time in the present. As I sank into roleplaying scenarios and sorrow, this song came on in the mix. The beat, the samples, the chords are simple and effective. As the title of the track repeated, I realized that...

"Hey...I don't have to have this experience. I can just..."

I'm glad that I was in a space to receive a message that was being sent. My mood lightened and I bopped my way through my evening.

I've also got another mantra/saying/slogan to help through these days.

Even if "House music/EDM isn't your thing"...this might be worth a quick listen.

Worked for me...
