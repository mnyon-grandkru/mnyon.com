Sometimes when I get home, I don't need to think. It helps to stare at a screen for a little, before getting into the night's activities.

One of the actors/comediennes I like to watch is Lisa Yaro. She had a few short sketches called Back handed compliments. Nice concept.

I also loved the bass and pad combination. The pause on the four at the beginning. I did some digging and found the original song. C'est un chanson francais! Incroyable!

I can be honest and say it almost doesn't matter what is being said here because the beat mesmerizes me. Nappyboy does have a voice that complements the track.

So check the song out, then check out Lisa, her sketches and her outfits. Peace.
