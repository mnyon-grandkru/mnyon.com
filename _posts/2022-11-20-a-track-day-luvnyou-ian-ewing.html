Been a minute. Going to clear my throat, get the rust out of my fingers & keyboard.

This joint and its creator Ian Ewing made it to my ears via Chillhop.

Just as the Inuit have 37 words for snow, dive deep into Downtempo/Lo-fi/Chillhop and over time, varieties of Chill become apparent. Some of these varieties aren't necessarily chill.

This song toys with the more somber end of the chill spectrum, but the steady chord progression moves the song along.

This single is likely present on a series of album, compilations and mixes.

The whole soundscape is dipped in a sea of reverb. A pleasant counterpart to my morning affirmation and planning.
