Wonderfully stilted semi-awkward song with reversed percussion. Not so wonderfully stilted review as a result of postus interruptus.

The reversed high-hats serve as a nod to the punk hip-hop connection back in the 80s. Or perhaps not.

The lyrics hit a bit differently in ones fourties than teens.

These jeans still kinda fit, even if the outfit looks different.

These horns still kinda mix, even in the light of the next day.

One question still fits though;

"Where did you get those eyes?"
