A track describing my favorite kind of people.

One of my favorite kinds of house.

Everyone's familiar with the kind of deep house that is mellow, contemplative. One can even study to it.

I don't know how many people are familiar with Masters At Work, but definitely check the resume.

This song isn't a "let's chill" song.

This is a "get your ass on this floor and see what we can make with our body movements."

One doesn't listen to this song to decipher the lyrics. The intent is clear.

"Get down, DOWN, down".

The beat has a bunch of syncopation for footwork to play with.

The voices have passion and connection, even in the hook.

I don't have much else to type, cause I need to dance now.

Enjoy,
