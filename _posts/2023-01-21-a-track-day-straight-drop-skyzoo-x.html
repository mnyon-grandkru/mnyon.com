Creativity is something sometimes.

I don't try to fight it with some things, so I'll post something here when I'm inspired.

I feel like the good thing about not remembering things, is that a thing that was a thing can be a thing again.

Enter Skyzoo, and new music.

I literally do not know who "The Other Guys" are. Based on what I hear so far, I need to learn more.

I feel like this song had a bunch of cheat codes; whether we're talking about Skyzoo's always fresh and consistent delivery, the choral chorus or the jazz bass, each of these does what's on their respective manuals.

Let's not forget the bars.

Plus a pean to the eighties with a reason to relay these

to the peeps who 'preciate me for the seasoning in stages.
