Once upon a time I had a bunch of judgemental opinions about music. Heh. One of those opinions had to do with Sade. I never believed that anyone should mess with her tracks.

The first few people who attempted justified my perspective.Some may be on SoundCloud

Oh how times have changed! Technology, music, me, all factors.

In the midst of a decentralization tech project, this song came up on my mix. This quickly became one of my new favorite walking songs while doing errands in NYC.

Kaytranada adds a steady drumbeat replete with echoing clap ("this beat is made for walking") and bar arrangement as the main discernible light touch on this remix. I could see this song in a Kiffen Beats mix.
