I think this is dope. Good to walk around town listening to this refreshing old school jam.

New year, old songs, refreshing to walk through the city with these sounds.

The video itself is nostalgic enough. The horns on the hook. The bass under Biz's words.

This reminds me that not every song needs to be super lyrical.

Reminiscing on good times. Reach out to those folk you haven't in a minute, y'all.
