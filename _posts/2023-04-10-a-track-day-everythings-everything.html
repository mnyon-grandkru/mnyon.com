"This is the part where you get in a circle!", "Move your body, move your body!"

These cats...damn.

Innercity Griots was one of those albums I was late on. Why? That. Damn. Single.

via GIPHY

...but I digress...

Freestyle Fellowship. Their picture's in the dictionary under "pioneer". This album was in steady rotation for several months. Each song has character. This one in particular, though?

The switch from call & response to rapid-fire chopping flows, the organic bassline. The mid song breakdown with their names (although the last one eludes me). The horn playing over the soundscape at the end. This is a traveling song, both the activity and the journey it takes.

I'm not going to describe too much more...but "everything is gonna be all right".

Long story short, this song is pretty cool.
