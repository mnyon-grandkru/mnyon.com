We've got a guitar lick, the voice and the drums. Each part plays its part, firmly ensconced in its role.

The guitar may have a slight flange on it, like a steel guitar whose strings are steadily and lightly tickled with a slow building energy.

The voice is steadfast, hitting each mark like a foot landing with military precision on the beat.

The beat is rhythm, and they all form a sparse yet filling soundscape.

The pockets that Asheru chose go well with the outfit. A knit cap on a light summer outfit.
