This is a nice summery track, even though it's a cloudy day.

I'm a fan of vocal arrangements interspersed throughout a track, and the chanting chorus delivers.

That and the melting ivories dripping onto a steady track make for a good time.

The voice is dappled in delay, echo and effect. Makes for a good combination.

I know I like a song when I add in my own effects.

It's been a minute. Glad to be back on it.
