Dylan Drazen began one of my favorite mixes with this song.

It's in my travel mix. Walking through airport terminals to the arpeggiated chords is a good pasttime of mine.

I bounce through the airport terminal, to my gate. Slump into my chair and fall asleep before the plane leaves the ground.

Funny, the journey doesn't feel difficult...
